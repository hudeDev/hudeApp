Burgdorf family welcomes you in the hotel and restaurant Burgdorf. Centrally located, close to train station and town center, public swimming pool behind the house. Modern, quiet (located to the rear) room with shower / toilet, free wireless Internet, mini-bar, flat screen TV and telephone. Conference rooms, bowling (with bowling mode), winter and summer garden, à la carte restaurant. Bikes on request. Dogs on request.
